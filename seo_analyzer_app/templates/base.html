SEO Analyzer

SEO Analyzer is a tool that helps you analyze your website and gives you a score based on the SEO best practices.

What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of optimizing your website to get organic traffic from search engines like Google, Bing, Yahoo, etc.

Features:

  • Basic SEO checks (title, meta description, headers, etc.)
  • Keyword rank checking (Google search position)
  • SEO score calculation (on-page SEO, keyword ranking, technical SEO)
  • Personalized SEO suggestions based on best practices

Usages:

  • Enter the URL of the website you want to analyze
  • Click on the "Analyze" button
  • Get the SEO score and personalized suggestions
